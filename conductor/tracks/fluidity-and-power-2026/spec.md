# Specification: Fluidity & Power 2026

## Goals
1. **Visual Immersion:** Make the UI adapt dynamically to the content being read.
2. **Extreme Performance:** Modernize the download engine and networking stack.
3. **On-Device Intelligence:** Strengthen offline capabilities for recommendations and organization.

## Features

### 1. UI/UX Refinements
- **Dynamic Theming:** Extract dominant colors from manga covers and apply them to the Reader and Details screens.
- **Smart Webtoon Detection:** Auto-switch to long-strip mode based on page aspect ratios.
- **Gesture Preview:** A visual editor for reader tap-zones and gestures.

### 2. Infrastructure
- **Multi-threaded Downloader:** Support parallel chunked downloads with resume capabilities.
- **Source Health Monitoring:** A dashboard to track and report the reliability of installed sources.

### 3. AI & Organization
- **Offline Recommendation Engine:** Privacy-first suggestions using local embeddings.
- **AI Clustering:** Automatic library categorization based on themes and genres.
