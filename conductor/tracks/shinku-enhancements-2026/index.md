# ShinKu Enhancements 2026

**Track ID:** `shinku-enhancements-2026`

## Description
This track covers a series of "immersive, intelligent, and personalized" enhancements for the ShinKu application, including advanced Gemini AI features, deep reading analytics, smarter library organization, and immersive reader improvements.

    *   Atmospheric Audio (Ambient genre-matching sounds)
    *   Predictive Pre-loading
5.  **Expanded Sync** ([Track Detail](./connectivity/index.md))
    *   WebDAV / Nextcloud Support
